Welcome to the Investment Fund Manager

This application helps you manage your investment portfolios efficiently. Below is a guide on how to use each section of the application:

1. Rebalance

The Rebalance section allows you to calculate how to allocate new investments to maintain your target asset allocation based on your age and glide path.

  • Enter the amount you wish to invest and your current holdings.
  • The application will suggest how much to invest in each fund to rebalance your portfolio.

2. Allocation Plot

The Allocation Plot displays a graphical representation of your asset allocation over time, based on your configured glide path.

  • Visualize how your allocations change as you age.
  • Use this to plan your long-term investment strategy.

3. Fund Performance

The Fund Performance section provides detailed performance metrics for your funds.

  • View current prices, daily changes, and historical returns.
  • Select individual funds to see their historical performance charts.

4. Edit Config

In the Edit Config section, you can customize your investment strategy.

  • Set your date of birth to calculate your age-based allocations.
  • Add or remove funds for each asset class and set their target percentages.
  • Configure your glide path by specifying asset allocations at different ages.

5. Create Fund

Use the Create Fund option to set up a new investment fund.

  • Define a unique fund name.
  • Configure funds and glide paths for the new fund.

Getting Started

To begin, select an existing fund from the dropdown menu or create a new one. Navigate through the sections using the navigation bar at the top.
